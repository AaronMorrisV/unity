FORMULA  1?

What actually is the Formula 1 in Schools Challenge?

F1 in Schools is a formula 1 themed technology competition students compete in teams of 3-6 as they are tasked with designing and manufacturing a scaled model F1 car as well as creating and resourcing many other entities including portfolios, pit display, sponsorship and team identity.

MAIN TASKS

Engineering

  • Aerodynamics research
  • Design and manufacture a model F1 car
  • Iterative testing and modification of car
  • Create a portfolio to document concepts, research and progress

Enterprise

  • Create a team identity:
    • - Logo
    • - Website
    • - Uniforms
  • Acquire sponsors
  • Create and market social media pages
  • Create a booth to showcase the team
  • Create an enterprise portfolio
  • Make an app! (Optional)

STEAM

Each year, 1.2 million students across the globe take part in the world’s largest STEAM competition, F1 in Schools.
It gives them a chance to develop and showcase their STEAM skills which have serious benefits for their future, real world career pursuits.
